Explore India: North, South, East, and West

North India: Manali

Discover the scenic beauty of Manali, nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh. A perfect getaway for adventure and nature lovers.

South India: Munnar

Visit Munnar, Kerala, renowned for its lush tea plantations and breathtaking hills, perfect for a peaceful retreat.

East India: Dawki

Explore Dawki in Meghalaya, with its pristine Umngot River and lush landscapes that provide a surreal experience.

West India: Udaipur

Discover Udaipur, Rajasthan, famous for its beautiful lakes and royal palaces, offering a glimpse into the rich history of India.
