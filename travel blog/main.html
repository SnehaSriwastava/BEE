Explore the Wonders of Morocco

From the vibrant souks of Marrakesh to the stunning Sahara Desert, Morocco is a land of rich history, diverse culture, and breathtaking landscapes. Whether you're hiking through the Atlas Mountains, exploring ancient medinas, or lounging by the coast, there's something for every traveler.

When to go

Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) are the most pleasant times, with warm days and cool nights. Summer (June-August) is stiflingly hot, except in the mountains, while winter can be chilly everywhere. Ramadan sees many restaurants close during the day and transport options can be limited.
